Choose your audience

Find the people who will love your business.

Your audience is here

Your audience is everywhere. With our powerful audience selection tools, you can target the people who are right for your business. Using what you know about your customers, such as demographics, interests and behaviours, you can connect with people similar to them. There are three options for choosing your audience on Wide Markets:

  • Main Audience
  • Categorized Audience
  • Retargeting Audience
Main Audience

Choose the people you want to reach

Whether you're a grocery store that wants more local customers or an online electronics retailer looking for people who are interested in your products, our Main Audiences targeting options allow you to reach people based on their demographics, location, living status and much more.

Demographics

Choose people based on age, gender, living status, education, workplace, job titles and more.

Location

Reach people in your radius, or where you want to do business.

Learn about using Main Audiences on Wide Markets
Categorized Audience

Get the people you need

Categorized Audience helps you find the people you want categorized by interests and behaviors. Targeting these people will make your advertising process much easier and productive.

Interests

Choose people according to their interests and lifestyle. It can be specific entertainment, favorite movies and more.

Behaviours

Find people according to their consuming behavior. Using our technology we can target in-store and online purchases.

Learn about Categorized Audience on Wide Markets
Retargeting Audience

Increase loyalty and customer base

Retargeting Audience gives you the ability to bring back leaving customers and increase their loyalty.

Based on Pixel Code

We can deliver your advertising and retarget your audience by embedding a piece of code in your website or landing page.

Based on contact list

By uploading your contact list of active customers, we can target them on the web and especially on social media.

Learn about Retargeting Audience on Wide Markets
